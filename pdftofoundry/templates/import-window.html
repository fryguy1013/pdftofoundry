Summary AP Standalone PFS Bounty Bestiary

You may import unmodified PDFs from Paizo only. For personal use only.

This will create actors/scenes/journal entries in this world. Importing flip mats/interactive maps will update maps for all matching scenes in the world.

Tokens will be placed in worlds for supported modules (indicated with a star). This is an early access feature and may not work in all cases.

Supported Adventures

  • Adventure Paths: Quest for the Frozen Flame #3 (and older)
  • Standalone: Night of the Gray Death (and older)
  • PFS: 3-13 Guardian's Covenant (and older, with some exceptions)
  • Bounties: #18 From Family Lost (and older)
  • One-Shots: #4 Mark of the Mantis (and older)

For more information about specific adventures, select a tab above
